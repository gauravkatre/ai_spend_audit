# Unit Economics

## What a Converted Lead is Worth to Credex

Credex sells discounted AI infrastructure credits. Assumption: average deal size is $2,000 in credits purchased, with a 20% margin = $400 gross profit per customer. If a customer renews once in 12 months, lifetime value = $800.

Conservative estimate: $400 per converted customer (single purchase, no renewal assumed).

## Customer Acquisition Cost by Channel

Hacker News post:
- Cost: $0
- Expected visitors: 500
- Audit completion rate: 20% = 100 audits
- Email capture rate: 35% = 35 leads
- Lead to consultation rate: 20% = 7 consultations
- Consultation to purchase rate: 30% = 2 customers
- CAC: $0 / 2 = $0 (time cost only, roughly 3 hours)

Reddit posts (3 subreddits):
- Cost: $0
- Expected visitors: 200
- Audits completed: 40
- Email captures: 14
- Consultations: 3
- Purchases: 1
- CAC: $0

Direct X outreach (20 DMs):
- Cost: $0, 2 hours of time
- Expected responses: 4
- Audits completed: 3
- Consultations: 1
- Purchases: 0 to 1
- CAC: $0 to minimal

Credex existing customer email:
- Cost: $0 (existing list)
- Expected opens: 40% of list
- Audits completed: 25% of openers
- Consultations booked: 15% of completions
- Purchases: 40% of consultations
- This is the highest converting channel due to existing trust

## Conversion Funnel Math

Audit completed to email captured: 35%
Email captured to consultation booked: 20%
Consultation booked to credit purchase: 30%

Overall: audit completed to purchase = 35% x 20% x 30% = 2.1%

To get 10 purchases: need 476 audits completed
To get 10 purchases at $400 each = $4,000 revenue from 476 audits

## What Would Have to Be True for $1M ARR in 18 Months

$1M ARR = $83,333 per month in recurring revenue
At $400 per customer (single purchase model), need 2,083 customers in 18 months = 116 per month

To get 116 purchases per month:
- Need 116 / 2.1% = 5,524 audits completed per month
- At 20% completion rate from visitors = 27,600 monthly visitors needed

Achieving 27,600 monthly visitors requires:
1. SEO content targeting "AI tool cost comparison" keywords — 3 to 6 month lag
2. Consistent HN and Reddit presence — 2 to 3 posts per month
3. Credex email list growing through referrals
4. Embeddable widget placed on 5 to 10 partner blogs about AI tools

This is achievable but requires the tool to go mildly viral at least once — one HN front page post drives enough word of mouth to sustain 5,000 to 10,000 monthly visitors organically.

The more realistic path to $1M ARR is increasing deal size — if Credex moves average purchase from $2,000 to $5,000 in credits, margin doubles and the visitor math becomes far more achievable.
