## FAQ

Q: Is this actually free?
A: Yes. No credit card, no signup required. You get your full audit result on screen immediately. We only ask for your email after you have seen your results, and only if you want the report sent to you.

Q: How accurate is the pricing data?
A: Every price in the audit engine is sourced directly from the vendor's official pricing page and verified as of May 2026. Sources are listed in our public PRICING_DATA.md file. We update pricing whenever vendors announce changes.

Q: What does Credex get out of this?
A: SpendSmart AI is a free tool built by Credex. If your audit shows significant savings, we may reach out to show you how Credex discounted credits can help you save even more. You are never obligated to buy anything.

Q: Is my data private?
A: Your email and company name are never shown on the public shareable link. Only tool names and savings numbers are visible publicly. We do not sell your data.

Q: What AI tools does the audit cover?
A: Cursor, GitHub Copilot, Claude, ChatGPT, Anthropic API, OpenAI API, Gemini, and Windsurf. We are adding more tools based on user requests.
